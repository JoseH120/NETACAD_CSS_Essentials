CSS Properties For Styling List

The List-Style-Type property

the list-style-type property defines the type of marker used for list items

  • disc reders a filled circle
  • decimal renders decimal numbers
  • circle renders an unfilled circle
  • square reders a square
  • lower-roman renders lowercase Roman numerls : i, ii, iii, iv,..
  • lower-alpha lowercase letters: a, b, c...
  • upper-alpha uppercase letters: A, B, C..., and more...

The List-Style-Position Property

The list-style-position determines whether the list marker appears inside or outside the list item's content box

The two corresponding values are:

  • inside - the marker appears within the content box(default).
  • outside - the marker appears outside the content box.
