align self property

allows you to override the align-items property for individual flex items. It's useful when you want to align a specific item differently from others in the same flex container.

You can aply align-self to any flex item to adjust its alignment along the cross axis, independent of the other items.

align self values
  • auto - inherits the align-items property of the container
  • flex-start - aligns the item at the start of the cross axis
  • flex-end - aligns the item at the end of the cross axis
  • center - aligns the item at the center of the cross axis
  • stretch - stretches the item to fill the container (default behavior)
